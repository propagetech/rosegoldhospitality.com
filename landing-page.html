ABOUT ROSEGOLD HOSPITALITY

Rosegold Hospitality is a hotel management company in India founded in 2016 with an aim of providing best-in-class management and advisories to individuals, branded and unbranded hotels, resorts and restaurants. With extensive experience of providing hospitality consulting services, we truly understand various hotel development stages and their specific requirements.

Whether you are building a new hotel property or re-branding an existing hotel, at Rosegold Hospitality, we can guide you to make an informed choice on your brand selection, project management and hotel commissioning. For operational assets, we can review the existing operations and suggest new ways to optimize costs and drive operational efficiencies and improve the top-line revenues thereby contributing to commercial success of your hotel investment.

PRINCIPLE CONSULTANT

Manoj Nandkeolyar
